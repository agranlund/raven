DS1307 replacement.
----------------------------------------------

MCP79410, MCP79411 is almost drop in replacement.
MCP79411 comes with pre-programmed mac-address.
MCP79410M does not have any eeprom, only sram.

only SW diff. SDA/SCK swap pins.
programming and registers etc of course.

(79411 variant removes the need for U407)
